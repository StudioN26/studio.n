The Waffle

The Waffle

Nestled in the picturesque hills of Kothagiri near Ooty, “The Peak” resort stands as a testament to innovative design and sustainable luxury. Its roof, gracefully crafted from the hyperbolic paraboloid curve, this avant-garde design seamlessly blends structural integrity with visual allure. . The resort’s open, natural floor plan boasts expansive spaces, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills. The dynamic silhouette of hyperbolic paraboloids serves as both an engineering marvel and a captivating design feature.
